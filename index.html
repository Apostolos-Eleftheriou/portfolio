A few words about myself.



I'm 28 years old, my love for computers and technology goes all the way back to when I was 9 years old. That was when I first saw a VT220 (a computer terminal which had only a screen and a keyboard). I was but a little kid stroking the keyboard pretending to be coding. That is my first memory as a child and quite frankly what later in my life proved to be what I actually love doing.

I got into computers and started building them for my friends, family and myself. I always was the go to tech guy, to fix a cellphone, a busted computer or a troublesome software..

Fast forward to 2020 I made the decision to improve my life for the better. I always was overweight.. and by 26 I was 140kg. So I did what I always do best, analyze, prioritize, study and learn about nutrition and hit the gym with a methodical plan to get the body I always wanted. Learned all about Calories, Macros you name it. A year later I had managed to lose a wooping 65kg and hit my goal weight of 75kg. I was the happiest man alive, but something was missing...

So I started this amazing journey with a curriculum named The Odin Project and with it I began learning how to expand upon my problem solving skills and learn HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I'm a very fast learner so I picked it up quite quickly... I'm continuing this never ending journey of knowledge and I want to keep improving on myself and my skills every day...
